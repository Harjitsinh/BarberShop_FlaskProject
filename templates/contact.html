Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers to Common Questions

Do I need to book an appointment?

While we accept walk-ins when possible, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred time slot and barber. You can easily book online through our website or call us directly at +1 940-612-9127.

What are your busiest times?

Weekends and evenings after 5 PM tend to be our busiest times. For a more relaxed experience, consider booking during weekday mornings or early afternoons. Thursday and Friday evenings are particularly busy, so we recommend booking these slots well in advance.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have free parking available in front of our shop and additional parking in the surrounding area. The lot is well-lit and monitored for your safety. Street parking is also available on Bell Avenue with no time restrictions during our business hours.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards for all our services. They can be purchased in-store or by calling us at +1 940-612-9127. Gift cards make perfect presents for birthdays, holidays, or special occasions. They are available in any denomination and never expire.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), cash, and mobile payment options including Apple Pay and Google Pay. We also offer contactless payment for your convenience and safety.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount to students with a valid student ID. This discount applies to regular haircuts and beard trims and is available Monday through Thursday. Please mention your student status when booking and present your ID upon arrival.
